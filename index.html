About the School

Narsingdi Government Girls High School aims to provide quality education to girls, empowering them with knowledge and skills for personal and professional success. The school fosters an inclusive and supportive environment where students are encouraged to excel academically and develop holistically.

Admission Process

  • Eligibility: Open to students completing primary education.
  • Application Form: Available at the school office or online.
  • Selection Criteria: Based on academic performance and entrance examinations.

Curriculum

The school follows the national curriculum set by the Education Board, offering subjects across Science, Humanities, and Commerce. The curriculum is designed to provide a balanced education that prepares students for their future studies and careers.

Extracurricular Activities

  • Cultural Programs: Students participate in various cultural events, including drama, music, and dance performances.
  • Sports: The school offers sports such as football, basketball, badminton, and athletics.
  • Debate Club: Fosters critical thinking and public speaking skills.
  • Science Club: Engages students in scientific exploration through experiments.
  • Literature Club: Encourages exploration of literature and writing contests.
  • Environmental Awareness Group: Focuses on sustainability initiatives.
  • Art and Craft Club: Encourages creativity through various art forms.
  • Community Service: Promotes social responsibility through community service projects.

Facilities

  • Library: A well-stocked library provides resources for students.
  • Laboratories: Science and computer labs equipped for practical learning.
  • Playgrounds: Ample space for sports and recreational activities.
